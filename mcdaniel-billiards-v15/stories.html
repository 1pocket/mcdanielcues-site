The Baltimore Bullet Commission

Commissioned to honor the 1980 billiards film The Baltimore Bullet, this one-off brings clean McDaniel geometry to a cinematic moment. Veneer stacks, disciplined ringwork, and a forearm tuned for feel — a bridge between cue lore and the silver screen.

Alicante ’99 — A World Title

In 1999, Nick Varner lifted the WPA World 9‑Ball Championship in Spain playing a McDaniel cue. The season remains a touchstone — when a champion’s nerve met a builder’s precision.
