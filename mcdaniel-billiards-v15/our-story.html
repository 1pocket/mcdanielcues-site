Our Story

Since 1971, Bill McDaniel has been crafting cues that transcend equipment and become heirlooms. Each McDaniel cue begins with hand-selected woods — birdseye maple, gaboon ebony — paired with precious materials like silver and ivory. Every taper is measured to the thousandth, every inlay set with jeweler’s precision.
