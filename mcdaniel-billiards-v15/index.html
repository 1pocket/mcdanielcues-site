Bespoke Heritage

Commissioned by champions, coveted by collectors. McDaniel cues are built like fine watches: precise, enduring, singular.

From the first billet to the final polish, every decision serves performance and beauty. Gaboon ebony, birdseye maple, ivory and silver inlays — materials chosen for stability and soul. Tapers honed to thousandths. Finishes cured for decades of play.

“Our production is finite. That’s how we protect the standard.”

The result is more than equipment. It’s a signature — an heirloom that feels alive in the bridge hand and true on the stroke.
