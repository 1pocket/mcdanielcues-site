About Us

Bill McDaniel began building cues in the late 1960s after growing up around the game in Jackson, Tennessee. For decades, McDaniel cues have been prized for their hit, balance, and meticulous fit and finish. Today, we work by appointment in Jackson as we document the archive and produce select new builds.
